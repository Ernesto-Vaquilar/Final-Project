About

Hey There!
I'm Ernesto Vaquilar (he/him)

I am a Gay, Mexican-Filipino American living in Fresno, California!
I love all things Tech, Art, and Animation, especially when those things come together.

As a kid, I gravitated towards all things animation: PBS Kids shows, Ghibli and Pixar films, The Simpsons, and the Nintendo 64 kiosk at my nearest Target store.

That last example sparked my curiosity in tech and in my school's computer lab. There, I would mess around in Kid Pix and learn to type.

Now, as a current Bitwise Industries Workforce student, I am learning about Web Development (with this being my very first website!).

And while technology and the Internet have come a long way, there is work to be done. Here's to a more inclusive, accessible, empathetic, and rationally compassionate future!
